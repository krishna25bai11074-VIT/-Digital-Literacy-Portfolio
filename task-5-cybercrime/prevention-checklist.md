# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist for College Students

## General Online Safety
1. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or password with anyone — 
   not even bank officials (real banks NEVER ask for this)
2. Always lock your phone with a strong PIN or biometric 
   authentication
3. Log out of banking and payment apps after every session
4. Do not click on unknown links received via SMS, WhatsApp, 
   or email
5. Regularly check your bank statements for unauthorized 
   transactions
6. Only download apps from official sources — Google Play 
   Store or Apple App Store

## Financial & UPI Safety (Mandatory Tips)
7. Remember — UPI is for SENDING money, never for RECEIVING 
   via QR code scan. If someone asks you to scan a QR code 
   to receive money, it is 100% a scam
8. Never accept requests to "verify" your UPI account over 
   a phone call — always call your bank's official helpline 
   directly to verify any issue

## Reporting
- If you are a victim of cybercrime, report immediately at:
  🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
  📞 Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7, free to call)
- Report within the first hour for the best chance of 
  freezing the fraudulent transaction
