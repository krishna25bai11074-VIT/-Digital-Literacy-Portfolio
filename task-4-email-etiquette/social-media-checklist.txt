# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ DO's
1. Keep your profiles professional — future employers WILL check them
2. Share your academic achievements, projects, and certifications
3. Follow industry experts and educators relevant to your field
4. Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable with your professor seeing it
5. Use privacy settings to control who sees your personal content

## ❌ DON'Ts
1. Don't share your phone number, address, or OTP publicly
2. Don't post offensive, discriminatory, or hateful content
3. Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying of any kind
4. Don't share unverified news or misinformation
5. Don't accept friend/follow requests from complete strangers
